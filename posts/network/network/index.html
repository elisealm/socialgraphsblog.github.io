The Whole Network

We have analyzed the episodes and seasons and the characters impact on them. The Graph shows the relations between all the characters ever participateed in the Gossip Girl series. The nodes are the characters, and they are also defined with their charater name. The edge between to nodes define that they have a relation, and the color of the edge define what type of relation. If the edge is grey, they are in family with each other, if it is pink they have a romantic relationship, if it is blue they are friends and if it is purple, they are enemies. The nodesize is dependent on the relevance in the series; The more episodes the character appear in, the bigger the nodesize.
