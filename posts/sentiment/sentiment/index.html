When analyzing the top 10 characters, we got the result which is visualized above in the graph. This shows the compound results for each of the characters. On the other hand side, Dan Humphrey or "Mr.Lonley Boy" has a very positive effect on his social circle. We can see that Jenny Humphrey and Nate Archibald are quite nuetral. We can think of the compound as a correlation. This means that is you hav a compound score of 1 it indicates that it is a highly positive sentiment and if you have a compound score of -1 it indicates that it is a highly negative sentiment.
