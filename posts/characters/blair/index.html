A. Blair Waldorf

Here you can take a look at Blair waldorf or “Queen B”. Blair has an ongoing up and down relationship with Chuck Bass, and after Serena van der Woodsen and Dan Humphrey. To take a closer look at the relations, we have made a graph and a wordcloud that visualizes them.

Blairs relations

This is the network of Blair. She has 64 number of relationships. As you can se, there are not 64 edges in the graph above, but she has several relationship types with the same people.

Wordcloud of spoken words

Since this is the description of the character, the most used word is, of course, the characters own name. We can also see that Chuck, Serena and Nate is mentioned quite alot. Chuck is her husband, Serena her bestfriend and Nate was her high school boyfriend and now her friend.
