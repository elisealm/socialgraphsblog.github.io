C. Dan Humphrey

Here you can take a look at Dan Humphrey or “Lonley Boy”. Dan has an on-again-off-again relationship with Serena van der Woodsen and the two have the longest running relationship on the show. Dan and Serena finally get married in the finale. Dan is good friends with Nate Archibald, Blair Waldorf, and Chuck Bass. To take a closer look at the relations, we have made a graph and a wordcloud that visualizes them.

Dans relations

This is the network of Dan. He has 46 number of relationships. As you can se, there are not 46 edges in the graph above, but he has several relationship types with the same people.

Wordcloud of spoken words

Since this is the description of the character, the most used word is, of course, the characters own name. We can also see that Serena, Chuck, amd Blair is mentioned quite alot. Serena is his wife, Chuck is enemy and friend, and Blair his girlfriend for a short period, friend and enemy.
