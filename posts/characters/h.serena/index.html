H. Serena Van Der Woodsen

Here you can take a look at Serena Van Der Woodsen, or New Yorks It-girl. Serena has an on-again, off-again relationship with Dan Humphrey and the two have the longest running relationship on the show. She is best friends with Blair Waldorf and shares close friendships with Nate Archibald and Chuck Bass. To take a closer look at the relations, we have made a graph and a wordcloud that visualizes them.
