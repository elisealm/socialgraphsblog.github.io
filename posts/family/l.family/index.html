L. he Families

In gossip Girl, there are alot of family drama and intrigues. The drama is about power, money and romantic relationships. The family drama has left us wondering, which of these families are actually the most powerful? We have therefore chosen to analyze this. We want to see which family is the most powerful, and which has the most contacts. The result is visualized in a network that shows amount of relations the three families have with the characters in gossip girl.

Bass Family

Edges in graph 39

The original Bass Family was made up of Bart and Chuck, and Chuch is adopted by Lily van der Woodsen. The whole family consists of: Bart Bass, Jack Bass, Lily Bass, Chuck Bass, Blair Bass, and Henry Bass.

Van Der Woodsen Family

Edges in graph 48

The van Der Woodsen family is one of the wealthiest families in Manhattan. The family is made up of Dr. William van Der Woodsen and his children, Serena and Eric van Der Woodsen, as well as his ex/current wife, Lily. Due to Lily’s and Serena’s realtion to both Rufus and Dan, the Van Der Woodsens are also connected to members of the Humphrey family. The whole family consists of: Serena Van Der Woodsen, Lily Van Der Woodsen, William Van Der Woodsen, Eric Van Der Woodsen, Serena Humphrey, Dan Humphrey, Chuck Bass, Blair Waldorf Bass, Henry Bass, Lola Rhodes.

Humphrey Family

Edges in graph 49

The Humphrey family consists of Rufus, Alison, Dan, and Jenny. The whole family consists of: Rufus Humphrey, Alison Humphrey, Dan Humphrey, Lily Bass Humphrey, Serena Humphrey, Milo Humphrey, Scott Rosson and Jenny Humphrey.

Result of the Analysis

The analyze prdictet that the most powerful family was The Humphreys. We can see that the number relations that the Humphreys has is 49, compared to the Van Der Woodsen with 48 relations, and on then last but not least the Bass family with 39 relations.
